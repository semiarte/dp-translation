services

localization / #l10n

My main area of specialization is video game and software localization.

Having researched in depth the topic of transcreation, I consider it an essential approach to provide video games with the right feeling. A localised videogame needs to give the same lasting impression in the target audience as the original would do, that is what I try to achieve.

Localization goes hand in hand with software and website translation. As a former computer sciences student, I’ve always understood software like an old friend of mine. The proof is in the pudding.

comic translation / #xl8

I am experienced with literary translation, in particular the translation of comics. As with every other creative content, I give comics the right tone and I ensure that the size of each globe is no limit to the imagination.

editing · proofreading

Even the best translator cannot avoid making a typo or two. Double-checking is a must, so I will give your translation a necessary second look.
